Samuel Groesch

I am a Research and Teaching Associate in the Media & Internet Governance Division at the University of Zurich, holding an MSc in Data & Society from LSE and a BA in Communication & Politics from Zeppelin University. My research interests include platform governance, data protection and the evaluation of harmful online content, as well as computational methods.
